We’re Austin and Casey, the founders of Community Love Project! We started this adventure for one simple reason: to share the love of Jesus with others. Our goal is to love on our community in a way that we can model an example for others to do the same! We want to help those in need while also sharing that love with others through social media as an inspiration for what they can do, too! We also love being able to offer opportunities for others to love on their community through our projects. This isn’t about us or what we do, but what happens in our hearts and in the hearts of others when we motivate each other to love!
